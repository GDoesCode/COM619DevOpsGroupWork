GDPR Guidelines Acceptance Policy

Welcome to Open-Source Mapping Application! We want to ensure you that you understand and agree to our application’s data protection practises that adhere to GDPR guidelines on how our Mapping application collects, use, disclose and protects your personal data while you use our application.

This project is Open-Source and may involve collaboration and contribution from the community, external developers may have access to this project but at Team NodeNinjas we are committed to protect our user’s personal data and maintain high data protection standards.

Data Collection

We collect and process personal data including Users saved Points of interests, GPS Location, Login Details and Cookies for the sole purpose of providing you with our applications mapping services.

By using our mapping application, you acknowledge and agree to the processing of your personal data mentioned in this policy.

By accepting these GDPR guidelines, you as the user give explicit consent to the processing of your personal data for the specific purposes for this Mapping Application. You have the right to withdraw your consent and erase your personal data at any time. To do so or for more information, please email our assigned Data Protection Officer Danny Agha at 5aghad30@solent.ac.uk

Data Security

Within our mapping application the team has implemented industry-standard security and encryption practises to protect your personal data from unauthorised access or misuse.

We are dedicated to maintaining data security measures in our open-source environment, including secure coding practises and encryption to protect your personal data.

Data Access

As a user of this mapping application, you have the right to request a copy off the personal data you have provided for the mapping application. Personal data may be shared within the open-source community for collaboration purposes. Such sharing of personal data will strictly adhere to GDPR guidelines.

Your personal data will only be stored and used by us, contributors to the open-source project are an integral part of our collaborative effort but bare bound by the same data protection standards and guidelines established by this project. Contributors access is limited to maintaining and improving the functionality of the project, ensuring the privacy and security of your personal data.

To request a copy of your provided personal data please email or assigned Data Protection Officer Danny Agha at 5aghad30@solent.ac.uk

Data Retention

We only retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this GDPR Acceptance Policy.

Over time the team may update this GDPR Acceptance Policy to reflect any changes within our data processing patterns or requirements.

If you have any questions, concerns or requests related to your personal data and our GDPR guidelines, please email our assigned Data Protection Officer Danny Agha at 5aghad30@solent.ac.uk with your enquiries.
